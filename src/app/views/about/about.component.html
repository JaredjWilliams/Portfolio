About Me

I began my software development journey while I was teaching a Preparing Colleges and Careers class at a local high school. Part of our curriculum was administering a career interest test to help students identify potential career paths post college or high school . I took the test myself and it suggested that I would be a good fit for a career in software development.

Mungomash Apprenticeship

I started working at Mungomash later that summer as an apprentice. In this role I was able to learn the basics of software development and web development through a series of projects.

Junior Mobile Developer

After my apprenticeship I was hired as a Junior Developer where I worked on a mobile application for a regional airline.

Fasttracked Program

My contract with the regional airline ended and I was offered a position in the Fasttracked program at Cook Systems. In this program I was able to refine my skills in software development and web development.
